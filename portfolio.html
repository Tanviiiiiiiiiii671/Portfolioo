Smart Home Automation System

An IoT-based system that allows users to remotely control home appliances and monitor indoor conditions like temperature, humidity, and air quality using a mobile app. It uses NodeMCU, DHT11, MQ-135, PIR sensors, and relays to automate appliances, provide real-time environmental data, and send alerts for unsafe conditions. An automated system that improves the safety, traceability, and efficiency of blood storage.
